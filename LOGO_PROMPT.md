# Logo Design Brief

The logo for 'awesome-dev-resources' in 8K Ultra-HD resolution and neo-glass style would feature a minimalist design with vibrant, cyberpunk-inspired colors. The logo would incorporate elements of glassmorphism, giving it a sleek and modern look.

The word 'awesome' would be prominently displayed in a futuristic font, with 'dev-resources' below it in a smaller, more subtle style. The overall design would be sleek and minimalist, with a focus on clean lines and geometric shapes.

The color scheme would include bright neon colors, such as electric blue, neon green, and hot pink, to give the logo a vibrant and eye-catching appearance. The use of glassmorphism would add depth and dimension to the design, making it feel modern and cutting-edge.

Overall, the logo for 'awesome-dev-resources' would be a striking and visually appealing representation of a forward-thinking and innovative brand in the world of development resources.
